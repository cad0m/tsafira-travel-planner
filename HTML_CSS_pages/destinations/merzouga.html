About Merzouga

Merzouga is a small village located in southeastern Morocco, known worldwide as the gateway to the stunning Erg Chebbi dunes. These majestic orange-hued sand dunes can reach heights of up to 150 meters and stretch as far as the eye can see, offering one of Morocco's most iconic desert landscapes.
