Moroccan Dining Tips

Dining Times

Moroccans typically dine later. Lunch is around 1-3 PM and dinner starts after 7 PM.

Eating Etiquette

It's customary to eat with your right hand. Many traditional dishes are shared from a common plate.

Mint Tea Culture

Mint tea is offered as a gesture of hospitality. It's polite to accept and sip slowly.
