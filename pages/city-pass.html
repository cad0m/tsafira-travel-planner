Frequently Asked Questions

How does the City Pass work?

The City Pass is a digital or physical card that gives you access to attractions and public transportation. Simply show your pass at the entrance of attractions or on public transport to gain entry.

When does my pass activate?

Your pass activates the first time you use it at an attraction or on public transportation. From that moment, it's valid for the consecutive number of days you've purchased.

Can I visit the same attraction multiple times?

Most attractions allow one-time entry with the City Pass. However, you can use public transportation as many times as you want during the validity period.

How do I receive my City Pass?

You can choose to receive your City Pass digitally via email or pick up a physical card at designated tourist information centers in the city.
